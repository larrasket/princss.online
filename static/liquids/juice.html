Strawberry lemonade is very much an all-time beverage for me. Strawberry and lemon? Are you kidding. Popeyes' is serviceable though, might be a little bit too sour for my liking -- I like my lemonade as sweet as can be without being too saccharine -- but it's still strawberry lemonade, so you can't really go wrong. Pairs well with their chicken too.

Rating:7.5/10
