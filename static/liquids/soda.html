Sparkling Ice Lemonade
Date logged: 07/21/2023

Two of my favorite things, carbonation and lemon. Unlike sprite which has more carbonation than it has lemon flavor (I don’t even taste the lemon in sprite) Sparkling Ice Lemonade has the perfect ratio between the two. The bubbles don’t assault your tongue, it’s more of a light prickle. Really good lemonade flavor, wish it was a little more citrus-y, but it still tastes like lemonade. Bought this on a whim and downed the fucker in 2 minutes.

Rating: 9/10
