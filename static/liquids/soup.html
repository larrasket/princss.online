Daisho Goma (Sesame) Miso Hot Pot Soup
Date logged: 03/01/2025

The broth is not as strong as maybe a sukiyaki broth, but it's still very good. Mixed with a little bit of soy sauce, this is a very good broth to pair with thin sliced pork and thin sliced beef. Been having a lot of fun with at-home hot pot, and this was a great addition to the list of broths to try.

Rating:7/10
