Koia Protein Cacao Bean
Date logged: 07/21/2023

Can't say I like protein shakes very much. Drinking them feels like drinking sand a little bit, the powder washing over your tongue is not something that I really enjoy. However, I can say that this protein shake is much better than its contemporaries. A lot of shakes have a distinctive flavor about them that tastes like whey, this one not so much. It really does taste like cacao so I'll give them that. Still a protein shake though. Only going to have this again because I'm a protein deficient husk.

Rating: 4/10
Frozen Strawberry Açaí Lemonade Starbucks Refreshers Beverage

A little bit too much ice, not enough strawberry açaí or lemon flavor. Still good though, very refreshing on a hot summer day like today. Again, a bit watered down for my taste, at least towards the end of drinking it. That's my least favorite part of drinking most smoothies: finishing it. Always feels like you're drinking its slushy, diluted remains. Even more so for this one.

Rating: 6.5/10
