Gold Peak Sweet Tea
Date logged: 02/27/2025

Drank some of this while I was working today. Not bad. Don't think I'm a fan of 'sweet tea' in general because the sweetness is kind of weak compared to the all-time GOAT tea, Texas sweet tea. This was pretty refreshing though and got me through the day. The Gold Peak Sweet Tea with Lemonade is way better, but this meets code.

Rating: 5/10
AriZona Sun Brewed Style Iced Tea with Lemon Flavor
Date logged: 07/13/2023

Really refreshing, especially on a hot day. I find that a lot of sweet tea, especially Texas Tea, is a bit too harsh in its sweetness. Also I automatically like anything that is lemon flavored, which makes this my favorite AriZona tea flavor out of all of them.

Rating: 8.5/10
