Propel Zero, Berry
Date logged: 02/26/2025

This was the refresher in my middle school gym class after class was over. Everyone would crowd in the men's locker room, right by the coach's office, and hand over $2 to Coach 'B' for a choice of Berry, Grape, or Strawberry Kiwi. I would either choose Berry or Grape. This drink has a lot of nostalgic attachment; I remember when I had already "graduated" middle school, I asked my Dad to get me a case of Propel to have at home. Now I drink it almost every day -- it's become a cornerstone of my life. Berry is good.

Rating: 8/10
